WordPress

Another aspect of this class was using sites such as WordPress and Squarespace to make our own sites. I found success in using Wordpress despite some initial confusion, and on this page I will explain my likes and dislikes with WordPress. I also will link my WordPress page for you to browse through.

Likes

  • It was easy to use and intuitive.
  • It had a lot of templates that were available for use.
  • I had a lot of control over what the pages looked like and what I could add in order to make them my own.
  • It was easy to add images and links in it.

Dislikes

  • Initially, it was challenging to create the home page.
  • Creating a domain name was confusing when it came to if you had to pay or not.
  • Publishing the site proved to be a bit of a challenge and also was confusing about payments.
